      2. Price negotiation template

      Thank you for your quotation. Your price is a little high for my market. If I buy in larger quantity, can you give me the lowest wholesale price? Please also tell me your best price for: 100 pieces 500 pieces 1000 pieces If the price is good, I can place a trial order first.

      3. Sample checking list

      • Product name and model are correct
      • Size, color and material match the order
      • Packaging is complete
      • No visible damage
      • Product works normally
      • Logo or label is correct if required
      • Photos/videos are saved before bulk order
      • Supplier confirms warranty or after-sale terms

      4. Shipping questions

      Please help me confirm the shipping details: 1. Can you ship to [Country] in Africa? 2. What is the shipping cost by air? 3. What is the shipping cost by sea? 4. How many days does it take? 5. Can you provide tracking number? 6. Do you support DDP / door-to-door shipping? 7. What documents are included?

      5. Payment risk reminders

      • Do not pay full amount to unknown supplier at the beginning
      • Ask for product photos, videos and company information
      • Start with sample or small trial order
      • Keep chat records and payment proof
      • Prefer platform transaction when possible
      • Compare at least 3 suppliers before bulk order
      • Check shipping cost before paying
      • Avoid unrealistic prices that are too cheap
